Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a typical project take? +
Most projects take 6-10 weeks from kickoff to launch. Timeline depends on scope, complexity, and how quickly we receive feedback. We provide a detailed schedule during our discovery phase.
What's your revision policy? +
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds (2-3 depending on tier). Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. We're committed to getting it right and work collaboratively to minimize extra rounds.
Do you work with clients remotely? +
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Video calls, shared documents, and clear communication make distance irrelevant. Some of our best partnerships are with remote clients.
What if I need changes after launch? +
All packages include post-launch support (30-90 days). After that, we offer maintenance retainers or hourly rates for updates. We'll never leave you stranded—think of us as your long-term partner.
Can you help with hosting and domain setup? +
Yes! We can handle everything from domain registration to hosting setup, or work with your existing providers. We'll recommend solutions that fit your needs and budget.
